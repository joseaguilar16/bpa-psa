Get In Touch With Your Local Animal Shelter!

If you're based in North Texas, then the SPCA is a great place to start with an education on pet animals! Our Website Design Team personally visited SPCA of Dallas to see the level of care the animals were getting, and we were surprised to see that it was top-notch! The environment was bright, the animals room were spacious, and we regularly saw employees taking the animals out for walks and playing with them.

Fun Facts about SPCA

  • SPCA of Texas has helped in getting more than 3000 animals adopted, many of which came from abusive homes.
  • SPCA of Texas has responded to 3000+ animal abuse/neglect calls each year, so don't be afraid to call if you notice suspicious activity.
  • SPCA of Texas offers cheap, professional clinic care for pets, including vaccinations, spay/neuter surgeries, heartworm prevention, and more!

How can you help?

One of the best ways to get involved with your community to help prevent animal abuse is to volunteer at local animal care facilities. These might be pet shelters, veterinary stations, etc! For those in the Dallas/Fort-Worth area, then the best place to volunteer is definitely SPCA! If you're of age, then you can help the facility with tasks such as cleaning the laundry for the animals, taking the animals on walks, feeding them, and much more! Animal abuse might be a daunting task to prevent worldwide, but we can tackle it one step at a time, starting within our own communities.
